Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Tellinex Limited ("Tellinex", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the My Tellinex app and our broadband services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, phone number, service address, and payment information when you sign up for our services. We also collect usage data including broadband speed test results, data usage statistics, and WiFi network information to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and maintain our broadband services, process payments, send service notifications, provide customer support, improve network performance, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for data storage with row-level security policies. Payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment providers (NCB, Scotiabank, Lynk) and is never stored on our servers.

5. Location Data

The My Tellinex app may request location access to find the nearest speed test server and to verify your service address. Location data is not shared with third parties and is only used to improve your service experience.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and optional analytics to understand how our app is used. You can opt out of analytics at any time through the app settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account by contacting support@tellinex.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The parental controls feature is managed by the account holder (parent/guardian).

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we retain anonymised data for up to 12 months for service improvement purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
